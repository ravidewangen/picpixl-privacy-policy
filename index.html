Privacy Policy

PicPixl (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the PicPixl mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

When you sign in to the app, we may collect limited personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo

This information is provided through Google Sign-In (OAuth).

2. Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information for analytics and app improvement purposes, such as:

  • Device information (model, operating system)

Images and Media

PicPixl does not allow users to upload or customize images.

The app displays pre-existing images that are stored on PicPixl’s servers. These images are provided only for viewing and sharing purposes.

Users can share these images using their device’s built-in sharing options (such as WhatsApp, Instagram, or other apps).

PicPixl does not track or store any additional data related to user-initiated image sharing.

Advertising & Analytics

PicPixl uses third-party services such as Google AdMob to display advertisements. These services may collect advertising identifiers, device information, and app usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not sell or rent personal information. Data may be shared only when required by law or for essential app functionality.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

To request account or data deletion, please email us at: picpixlapp@gmail.com
