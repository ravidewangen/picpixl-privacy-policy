Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

If the app uses Google Sign-In, we may collect limited personal information such as:

  • Name

  • Profile photo

This information is provided through Google Sign-In (OAuth) and is used only for basic app functionality.

2. Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal information for analytics and app improvement purposes, such as:

  • Device information (model, operating system)

Images and Media

PicPixl provides a collection of ready-made marketing and greeting images that are hosted on secure servers, including third-party services such as Supabase, and displayed within the app for viewing and sharing purposes.

Users may optionally select a personal photo from their device gallery to include in designs for marketing or greeting use.

Personal photos selected by users remain on the local device and are processed only on the device. The app does not upload, store, or transmit user-selected personal photos to any server.

PicPixl does not access, collect, or retain personal photos selected by users. Users can share marketing images using their device’s built-in sharing options (such as WhatsApp, Instagram, or other apps).

PicPixl does not track or store any additional data related to user-initiated image sharing.

PicPixl uses third-party services such as Google AdMob to display advertisements. These services may collect advertising identifiers, device information, and app usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.
